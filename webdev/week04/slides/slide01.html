Ralph McQuarrie



  • American concept artist and painter
  • Born in 1929 in Gary, Indiana
  • Served in the Korean War and survived being SHOT IN THE HEAD.
  • Did illustrations for Boeing, specifically the 747
  • Illustrated for CBS's coverage of the Apollo landings
  • Basically defined the entire visual style of the Star Wars series from the beginning
  • Also worked on Close Encounters of the Third Kind and E.T.
