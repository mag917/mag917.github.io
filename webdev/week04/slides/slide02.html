Darth Vader

The Man Behind The Mask

George Lucas, in the early drafts of the script for Star Wars, only described Vader as having a flowing black cloak and a samurai-esque helmet.
It was Ralph McQuarrie's idea to give him the iconic mask and breathing device. as he was shown jumping between ships in these early drafts.
The whole "jumping between ships" didn't make it into the movies, but he DID ride on top of his Tie Fighter in one of the animated TV shows.
